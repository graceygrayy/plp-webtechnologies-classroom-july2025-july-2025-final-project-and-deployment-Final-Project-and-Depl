FAQ

1. How long do oil perfumes last?

Our perfumes can last 8–12 hours depending on the fragrance and your skin type.

2. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide with fast and reliable delivery options.

3. What makes oil perfumes different from sprays?

Oil perfumes are alcohol-free, making them longer-lasting and more skin-friendly.
