Our Story

Handcrafted oil perfumes designed to celebrate timeless elegance and individuality.

About Us

Gracey Oil Perfume began from a love of classic fragrances and the desire to create affordable luxury. We blend premium oils and natural extracts to craft long-lasting scents that tell a story.

Whether you prefer floral, woody, or oriental notes, each bottle is carefully blended and bottled with care. We believe a perfume should be personal, memorable, and true to you.

Our Mission

At Gracey Oil Perfume, our mission is to make luxury fragrances accessible to everyone. We aim to create scents that inspire confidence and bring elegance into everyday life — crafted with passion, authenticity, and excellence.
